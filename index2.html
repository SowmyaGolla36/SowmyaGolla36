Types of Fruits



1.Fruits
Fruits are the sweet and fleshy parts of plants that contain seeds. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them essential for a healthy diet. Common fruits include apples, bananas, mangoes, grapes, and oranges. Eating fruits regularly helps boost immunity and improve digestion.

2.Snackable Cups
Snackable fruit cups are small, ready-to-eat servings of cut or mixed fruits packed in cups. These cups are convenient, healthy snacks, especially for kids, students, and office-goers. They often include fruits like pineapple, papaya, watermelon, apple, grapes, or mango in bite-sized pieces. Some may come with juice or light syrup. Usefulness: Healthy alternative to chips or junk food. Portable and easy to carry in lunch boxes or bags. Time-saving – no need to cut or wash fruits. Great for portion control – helps avoid overeating.
3.Cut Fruites
Cut fruits are fresh fruits that are peeled, chopped, and ready to eat. They are convenient, quick, and healthy snack options. Common cut fruits include watermelon, papaya, apple, pineapple, and musk melon. They are perfect for busy people who want nutrition without the hassle of cutting.
