Types of Flowers



1.TULIP
The tulip is a brightly colored, cup-shaped flower that blooms in the spring. It comes in many colors like red, yellow, pink, and white. Tulips are known for their simple beauty and are often grown in gardens and used in floral arrangements. They symbolize love, elegance, and renewal.
2.ROSE
The rose is a beautiful, fragrant flower known for its layered petals and variety of colors like red, pink, white, and yellow. It is often associated with love, romance, and beauty. Roses are widely used in gardens, bouquets, and perfumes, and they hold cultural and symbolic meanings in many traditions.
3.HIBISCUS
Hibiscus is a colorful, trumpet-shaped flower known for its large, vibrant petals. It grows in warm climates and comes in many colors like red, pink, yellow, and white. Hibiscus is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicines, and it attracts butterflies and bees.
3.Lily
Lily is a beautiful, fragrant flower with large, elegant petals that come in colors like white, pink, orange, and yellow. It symbolizes purity and renewal and often blooms in spring or summer. Lilies are commonly used in bouquets and decorations.
