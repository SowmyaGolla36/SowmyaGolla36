Types of Vegetables



1.Vegetables
Vegetables are edible parts of plants such as roots, stems, leaves, and flowers. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, helping to maintain good health. Eating a variety of vegetables daily boosts immunity and supports digestion.
2.Leafy Vegetables
Leafy vegetables are the edible leaves of plants. They are rich in iron, calcium, fiber, and vitamins like A, C, and K. Examples include spinach, lettuce, and fenugreek. They help in boosting immunity and improving digestion.
